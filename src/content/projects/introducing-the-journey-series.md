I have been searching for a good tag-line that would most appropriately tell the story of what I was hoping to do here. I feel that every project, whether it be electronics, modeling, or design, involves some type of journey along the way.

I have been adding blog entries for completed projects, but I am now also adding entries for problems along the way the help people avoid the pitfalls and issues I encountered along the way. The blog entries can be found under the "Journey" tag.
